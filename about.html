About

Cluett Carpentry is an East London based cabinetry company specialising in bespoke furniture. The company is run by Dan, the propriertor. He has worked in the trades for the past ten year in carpentry, building, tiling, plastering but now concentrates exclusively on furniture. He works with a small team of makers and artists and brings an interesting holistic view to furniture production.
